DESIGN STYLE EXPLORATION

Personal · Visual Exploration

Ongoing personal project exploring Brutalism, Neo-Brutalism, and Experimental UI. Studying typography, contrast, grid-breaking layouts, and bold color to build a recognizable personal design language.

"The Infinite Fall" - A graphic design exploration of memory and trauma through monochromatic visual storytelling. This piece features falling figures against architectural elements, with integrated typography that creates vertical motion and emotional depth.

"Modern Exploration" - A contemporary take on experimental design principles, blending modern aesthetics with brutalist influences to create visual tension and dynamic compositions.

Both works explore themes of memory, trauma, and psychological weight through visual metaphor. The contrasting approaches demonstrate the versatility of experimental design language across different emotional contexts.
